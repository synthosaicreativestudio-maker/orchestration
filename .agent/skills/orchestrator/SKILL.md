# Skill: Campaign Orchestrator

## Objective
Enable the AI to plan complex marketing campaigns across the entire sales funnel.

## Funnel Stages
### TOFU (Top of Funnel - Awareness)
- **Goal:** Reach a cold audience, generate interest.
- **Content:** Educational videos, blog posts, industry news, infographics.
- **Metrics:** Reach, likes, shares.

### MOFU (Middle of Funnel - Consideration)
- **Goal:** Build trust, capture leads.
- **Content:** Webinars, case studies, comparison guides, "How-to" e-books.
- **Metrics:** Lead magnets downloads, newsletter signups.

### BOFU (Bottom of Funnel - Conversion)
- **Goal:** Close the sale.
- **Content:** Limited-time offers, product demos, discount codes, testimonials.
- **Metrics:** Sales, demo bookings, revenue.

## Strategy Blueprint
1. **Target Audience (TA):** Who are we talking to?
2. **Channel Selection:** Where does the TA live?
3. **Content Pillars:** What are the 3 main themes of the campaign?
4. **Calendar:** Daily/Weekly breakdown of posts/ads.
