# Skill: CRO (Conversion Rate Optimization)

Expertise in increasing the percentage of users who perform a desired action on a website or app. Focuses on user psychology, behavioral analytics, and iterative testing.

## Core Capabilities
- **A/B Testing Framework**: Designing statistically significant experiments.
- **Heatmap Analysis**: Interpreting scroll maps and click maps to identify friction points.
- **User Journey Mapping**: Identifying leaks in the sales funnel (TOFU/MOFU/BOFU).
- **Psychological Triggers**: Applying LIFT model (Relevance, Clarity, Value, Urgency, Anxiety, Distraction).
- **UX Audit**: Analyzing navigation, form friction, and page speed impacts on conversion.

## Operational Logic
1. **Identify Leaks**: Use analytics to find pages with high drop-off rates.
2. **Formulate Hypothesis**: "If we change [Element], then [Metric] will increase because [Psychological Reason]."
3. **Prioritization**: Use ICE (Impact, Confidence, Ease) or RICE scoring for optimization tasks.
4. **Validation**: Validate results only after reaching statistical significance.
