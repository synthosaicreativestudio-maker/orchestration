# Skill: Media Buyer (Ads Optimizer)

Expertise in managing and optimizing digital advertising campaigns across Facebook Ads, Google Ads, and other platforms. Focuses on ROAS, CPA, and efficient budget allocation.

## Core Capabilities
- **ROAS Calculation**: Precise calculation of Return on Ad Spend using the formula: `(Revenue from Ads / Cost of Ads) * 100`.
- **Bid Management**: Strategy for manual and automated bidding (Target CPA, ROAS, Max Conversions).
- **Campaign Structure**: Implementation of Hagakure (Google) or Power5 (Facebook) structures.
- **Budget Scaling**: Rules for scaling successful campaigns without resetting the learning phase.
- **Attribution Analysis**: Analyzing first-click, last-click, and data-driven attribution models.

## Operational Logic
1. **Audit First**: Before changes, audit current CPC, CTR, and Conversion Rate.
2. **Calculate Break-even ROAS**: Determine the minimum ROAS needed to remain profitable.
3. **Optimize Creatives**: Analyze creative performance and propose A/B tests for "Hook" and "Value Proposition".
4. **Keyword/Audience Refinement**: Identifying negative keywords and high-performing interest clusters.
