# Skill: ToV (Tone of Voice) Extractor

Expertise in analyzing existing content to define and replicate a brand's unique communication style. Focuses on linguistic patterns, sentiment, and personality traits.

## Core Capabilities
- **ToV Audit**: Analyzing sample texts for attributes (Formal vs. Casual, Serious vs. Humorous, etc.).
- **Lexicon Mapping**: Identifying "Power Words" to use and "Forbidden Words" to avoid.
- **Syntactic Analysis**: Determining preferred sentence structures (e.g., Short/Punchy vs. Long/Sophisticated).
- **Style Matrix Creation**: Defining 4-5 core pillars of the brand persona.
- **Consistency Check**: Auditing new copy against the established ToV matrix.

## Operational Logic
1. **Input Analysis**: Scan the representative text provided by the user.
2. **Pattern Identification**: Extract recurring adjectives, metaphors, and greeting styles.
3. **Define Persona**: Describe the brand as if it were a person (e.g., "The Encouraging Mentor").
4. **Create Guidelines**: Formulate "Dos and Don'ts" for future content.
