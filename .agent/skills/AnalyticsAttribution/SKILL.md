# Skill: Marketing Analytics & Attribution (GA4/GSC)

Expertise in tracking, analyzing, and reporting on marketing performance using GA4, Google Search Console, and attribution modeling.

## Core Capabilities
- **GA4 Event Strategy**: Defining custom events and conversions (Key Events) for tracking the user journey.
- **Attribution Modeling**: Comparing Last-Click vs. Data-Driven models to understand true ROI.
- **GSC Performance Analysis**: Finding low-hanging fruit in search results (high impressions, low CTR).
- **Funnel Visualization**: Identifying bottlenecks in the checkout/lead gen process.
- **Calculated Metrics**: Creating custom formulas for CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost) and LTV (Life-Time Value).

## Operational Logic
1. **Source Audit**: Verify if all traffic sources are correctly tagged with UTMs.
2. **Goal Alignment**: Confirm if the recorded conversions match actual business outcomes.
3. **Anomaly Detection**: Look for sudden spikes or drops in traffic and identify the cause.
4. **Actionable Insights**: Provide 3 concrete steps to improve conversion based on the data.
