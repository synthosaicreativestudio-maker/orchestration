# Skill: Marketplace Specialist (Amazon/WB/Ozon)

Expertise in optimizing presence and sales on major marketplaces. Focuses on "Rich Content," listing SEO, and platform-specific internal marketing.

## Core Capabilities
- **Listing SEO**: Optimizing titles, bullet points, and descriptions for marketplace algorithms (A10, etc.).
- **Image/Video Strategy**: Planning main images for CTR and A+ Content/Rich Content for conversion.
- **Internal Ads Manager**: Managing PPC campaigns within the marketplace (Sponsored Products, Brands).
- **Review Strategy**: Legal methods for improving rating and handling negative feedback.
- **Inventory & Pricing**: Analyzing competitor pricing and advising on stock levels vs. velocity.

## Operational Logic
1. **Competitive Audit**: Check top-3 competitors for keyword density and image quality.
2. **Identify Primary Benefit**: Determine what key feature drives sales in this niche.
3. **Optimized Draft**: Create a listing draft that balances SEO (for the bot) and persuasion (for the human).
4. **Ads Setup**: Propose initial campaign structure for rapid indexing.
