# Skill: Direct Marketing & Copywriting

## Objective
Enable the AI to generate high-conversion marketing copy using proven psychological frameworks.

## Frameworks
### AIDA (Attention - Interest - Desire - Action)
- Best for: General advertising, landing pages, email marketing.
- Execution: Start with a hook, build curiosity, highlight benefits, clear CTA.

### PAS (Problem - Agitation - Solution)
- Best for: Social media ads, sales letters, addressing customer "pain".
- Execution: Identify a pain point, make it feel urgent/unbearable, present the product as the cure.

### BAB (Before - After - Bridge)
- Best for: Case studies, short social posts, testimonials.
- Execution: Show the "broken" state, visualize the "dream" state, explain how the brand gets them there.

## Rules
1. **Know the Persona:** Always ask or search for the Target Audience (TA) before writing.
2. **Hook Integrity:** The first sentence must stop the scroll.
3. **Benefit over Feature:** Don't say "It has 500 lumens", say "It lights up the darkest alleys".
