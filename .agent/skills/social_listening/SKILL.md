# Skill: Sentiment & Social Listening

## Objective
Analyze brand perception and competitor strategies using real-time data.

## Methodology
### 1. Competitor Auditing (Web Search Required)
- Identify top 3-5 competitors.
- Analyze their content frequency, tone, and engagement.
- Identify "market gaps" (questions users ask that competitors don't answer).

### 2. Sentiment Analysis
- Categorize mentions/comments as Positive, Neutral, or Negative.
- Identify "red flags" (critical bugs, PR crises).
- Track "Brand Sentiment Score" over time.

### 3. Trend Spotting
- Monitor hashtags and keyword volume.
- Detect "viral" topics early for newsjacking opportunities.
